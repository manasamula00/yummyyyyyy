Food and Wine Pairings

1. White Meat (Chicken, Turkey): Opt for medium-bodied white wines like Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, or Viognier. These wines provide a refreshing balance to the lighter flavors of white meat. 2. Seafood: For delicate fish dishes, choose light and crisp white wines such as Pinot Grigio, Sauvignon Blanc, or Riesling. If the seafood is richer or has a buttery sauce, consider a medium-bodied white wine like Chardonnay or a dry rosé.
